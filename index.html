COLOUR BLAST

Shoot down as many enemies as possible to rack up the points and avoid the enemy bullets in the process.

HOW MANY POINTS CAN YOU SCORE?!

LEVELS:

EASY: There will be 5 enemies and you have 5 lives.

NORMAL: There will be 7 enemies and you have 3 lives.

HARD: There will be 10 enemies and you have 1 life.

Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys or the A and D keys to move. Use the SPACEBAR to shoot.

Click on the PLAY AREA to PAUSE/UNPAUSE
